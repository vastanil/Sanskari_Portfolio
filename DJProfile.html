DJ SANSKARI
Official Website

ABOUTo

Sanaskari, a DJ and dance music producer from Pune, has cross-genre love for music, blending varied modern music genre with pop and scoring hits in collaboration with a wide range of artists. His favourite genre include bollywood, house, trap, moombahton and trance. DJ Sanaskari brings forth a vision that is elegant and playful synchronously. His career quickly gained recognition for his remixes of numerous pop hits. Starting as a DJ and remixer, in his journey in the music world, he is bound to become one of the most significant artists in the industry.

List of artists that he has shared console with include : DJ LEMON; DJ SHREYA; DJ ABHISHEK; DJ DUSTIN; DJ SUNNY HURNEET; DJ ATUL; DJ RFACTOR; DJ NINOCUE; DJ AKEY; DJ ADDA; DJ ART; DJ KUSH; DJ LESH INDIA; DJ LALIT; DJ KAUR; DJ KDBELLE; DJ VISPI.

He has headlined a few festivals establishing himself as a prodigious talent, forward-thinking producer, dynamic DJ. With his recent releases on youtube, which have garnered several thousands views and likes, DJ Sanaskari has set the tone for what is yet to come. He has a long experience of getting crowd to grove to his beats on the dance floor.

He has been playing his music at famous party places in Pune like : PUBLIC , KP; BETO'S BAR AND KITCHEN; THE LIQUOR GARAGE, PUNE; ORILLA, CDC PUNE; PRYSM, HINJEWADI; ANDROMEDA, BANER; TALLI, BANER; CHEERS, KHARDI; FLY HIGH, KHARDI; SKY HIGH, KHARDI; TOSS, KHARADI; CAFE 1730 , KHARDI; HIGHLAND, NIBM; HIGHLAND, KALYANI NAGAR; ORIANO, BANER; BARBACOA, BHUGAO; FLY HIGH, YERWADA; The Tenth Floor; for almost four years now.
